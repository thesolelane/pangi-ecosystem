## üé® Bootstrap Artwork Strategy

### Option 1: AI-Generated (Cheapest)
**Cost**: $30-100
**Tools**: Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion
**Process**:
1. Create base pangopup/adult designs
2. Generate variations with AI
3. Manual touch-ups in Photoshop/GIMP
4. Create trait layers

**Pros**: Very cheap, fast
**Cons**: Less unique, AI stigma

---

### Option 2: Budget Artist (Best Value)
**Cost**: $1,000 - $3,000
**Where**: Fiverr, Upwork, Twitter artists
**Process**:
1. Find artist with NFT experience
2. Commission base designs + traits
3. They generate variations
4. You handle metadata

**Pros**: Original art, professional
**Cons**: Takes time, quality varies

---

### Option 3: Revenue Share Partnership (No Upfront Cost)
**Cost**: $0 upfront, 10-20% of mint revenue
**Process**:
1. Find hungry artist wanting exposure
2. Offer 15% of mint revenue
3. They create all artwork
4. Pay after successful mint

**Pros**: Zero upfront cost
**Cons**: Hard to find, artist takes risk

---

### Option 4: Hybrid Approach (Recommended)
**Cost**: $500 - $1,500
**Process**:
1. Commission base designs from artist ($500)
2. Use AI to generate variations
3. Manual cleanup and trait mixing
4. Create 3,000 unique pieces

**Pros**: Affordable, unique, scalable
**Cons**: Requires your time
