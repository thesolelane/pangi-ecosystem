### 1. Set Up Your Wallet

You'll need a Solana wallet to interact with PANGI:

### 2. Get Devnet SOL

PANGI is currently on Solana Devnet for testing:
1. Visit [Solana Faucet](https://faucet.solana.com/)
2. Enter your wallet address
3. Request 1-2 SOL (free testnet tokens)

### 3. Connect to PANGI dApp

1. Visit the PANGI dApp (URL provided by deployment)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top-right
3. Select your wallet (Phantom/Solflare)
4. Approve the connection

### 4. Get PANGI Tokens

**Option A: Airdrop (Devnet)**
- Contact the team for test tokens
- Tokens will be sent to your wallet

**Option B: Mint NFT**
- Navigate to "Mint" section
- Click "Mint PANGI NFT"
- Approve the transaction
- Your NFT starts at "Egg" tier
